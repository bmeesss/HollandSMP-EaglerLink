Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This privacy policy explains what data hollandSMP collects when you visit our website (hollandsmp.nl) or Minecraft server, and how this data is used. hollandSMP is a hobby project and not a registered company.

Who is responsible for your data

hollandSMP is operated by Mason Coemaet. For questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us via our Discord server.

What data we collect

Website (via Google Analytics)

  • Anonymized IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website (where you came from)

Website (via Google AdSense)

  • Cookies and similar technologies used to serve and measure ads
  • Possibly personalized or non-personalized ad data, depending on your cookie preference

Minecraft server

  • Your in-game username and player ID
  • Chat messages and in-game actions, for moderation purposes
  • Connection data (such as IP address) for anti-cheat and security

Why we use this data

  • To make the website and server function technically
  • To understand how visitors use the site and to improve it
  • To show ads through Google AdSense, which helps cover server costs
  • To keep the server safe and fair (moderation, anti-cheat)

Cookies

We use cookies from Google Analytics and Google AdSense. You can set your preference via the cookie notice on the site, or disable cookies in your browser settings. More information about how Google uses data can be found at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Advertising through Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ad Settings.

Sharing data with third parties

We do not sell your data. Anonymized or aggregated data is shared with Google (Analytics and AdSense) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Retention period

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default settings. In-game data is retained for as long as needed for moderation and server management.

Your rights

As an EU resident, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Please contact us via our Discord server to exercise these rights.

Minors

Our server is open to players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect additional personal data from minors beyond what is described above, and we encourage parents to supervise their children's online activity.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date at the top.
